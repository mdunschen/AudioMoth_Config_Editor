About the App

The AudioMoth Config Editor is an Android app designed to simplify the process of configuring your AudioMoth device. Whether you're in the field or at home, you can adjust settings like sample rate, recording schedules, frequency filters, and amplitude thresholds—all without needing a laptop.

This app covers all the essential features of the official Windows app, but with the convenience of mobile access. Perfect for researchers, wildlife monitors, and hobbyists alike.

Key Features

Sample Rate Configuration

Set the sample rate to match your project's requirements.

Recording Schedule

Define custom schedules for when your AudioMoth should record.

Frequency Filtering

Apply filters to focus on specific frequency ranges.

Amplitude Thresholds

Set thresholds to trigger recordings based on sound levels.

Field-Ready

No laptop? No problem. Configure your device on the go.

User-Friendly Interface

Intuitive design for quick and easy setup.

How to Use

  1. Connect your AudioMoth device to your Android phone or tablet via USB using a USB OTG adapter.
  2. The app detects your AudioMoth automatically and opens the settings editor.
  3. Adjust the settings as needed.
  4. Apply the configuration to your device.
  5. Save/Load a config to/from your phone or tablet's local storage (Internal storage or micro SD.

For detailed instructions, refer to the user manual (coming soon).
